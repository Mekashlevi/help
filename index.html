Tacos

This Recipe is made by Me

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 cup of mexican blend cheese
  • 1 box of hardshell tacos

Directions

  1. Measure out the 1/4 cup of mexican blend cheese and 2 tablespoon of oil and set aside.
  2. Dice the 1/2 of white onion and set aside.
  3. Mince the cloves of garlic and set aside.
  4. Grab the medium pan and pace on stove setting it to medium and put the 2 tablespoon of oil and let it heat up
  5. Once the pan and oil are heated, put in the diced 1/2 white onion and mix. Cook the white onion for 5 mins jsut until it is softened
  6. After the onions of softeneed, place the 1 pound of ground beef. DO NOT BREAK IT UP. Setting the grond beef for a few mins in he pan will ensure browning of the meat. Brown for 2-3 mins
  7. After the meat is browning, place the 2 cloves of garlic, salt, pepper, and taco seasoning in the pan. Mix wel.
  8. Cook the meat for about 7 more mins ensuring that the meat is well cooked. Taste and adjust the meat to your liking.
  9. After the meat is done cooking, place a hardshell taco on a plate and scoop some of the taco meat into the hardshell as much as you'd like. Place some of the mexican blend cheese on top of the taco meat and you are finished!
